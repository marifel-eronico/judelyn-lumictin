ABOUT US

At JL Grand Hotel, we believe that every stay should be a seamless blend of comfort, luxury, and personalized service. Nestled in the city of Talibon, Bohol Philippines, our hotel is designed to offer a sophisticated retreat for both business and leisure travelers alike. From elegantly appointed rooms and suites to our scenic rooftop lounge and even to our delicious foods and drinks and others every detail at JL Grand is crafted to exceed your expectations.

JL Grand was founded on a passion for hospitality and a dedication to creating unforgettable guest experiences. From the moment you arrive, our team is committed to ensuring you feel welcomed and at home. With a rich heritage of commitment to sustainability, we bring a personal touch to every aspect of your stay.
